Are you interested in

- helping patients breathe using advanced technology
  - e.g., teach patients, give meds, run vents at up to 900 breaths per minute
  - What is an RT?
- a health care job after a 2 year college degree
- a college program with
  - small classes
  - high board pass rates
  - serious education for serious students who are preparing for careers
  - non-profit state school: we focus on your success, not on our profit 
  - inexpensive for Florida residents, competitively priced for students from other states
  - while having ... a popular salt water wind surfing location on the edge of campus
- living in a beach resort town on the Florida Gulf Coast
  - where the water is generally warmer than on the Atlantic coast
  - where people can often find cheaper housing, off the beaten path, in our "off season"
  - where Spring Break happens
- the potential to go on to finish a BS degree in anything, anywhere, at any college
  - while always having an employable skill
    - earn RT wages instead of fast food wages, when moonlighting to pay for your BS degree
  - consider a BS in engineering, pre-medicine, business, whatever
  - even take a chance on a BS degree where jobs are scarce
    - be employed as an RT while building a parallel career (e.g., a non-starving artist)
